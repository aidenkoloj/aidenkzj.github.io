Marble Making

In 2021 I went on a solo bike trip from Boston to Denver. Along the way, somewhere in a small town in Kentucky, I came across a local music festival with some vendors. One vendor was selling what looked like mushrooms encased in glass.

After discovering that the MIT makerspace has glass flameworks, I experimented with recreating these mushrooms in glass marbles.
